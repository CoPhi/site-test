Historical Milestones Timeline

[Image of a timeline illustration]
  • 1903

    First Controlled Flight

    The Wright brothers achieve the first sustained flight of a heavier-than-air powered machine.

    This event marked the beginning of the aviation age and profoundly changed travel and warfare.

  • 1928

    Discovery of Penicillin

    Alexander Fleming discovers the first antibiotic, Penicillin, revolutionizing medicine.

    Penicillin dramatically reduced the death toll from bacterial infections and injuries.

  • 1969

    Moon Landing

    NASA's Apollo 11 mission successfully lands humans on the Moon for the first time.

    Neil Armstrong was the first man to step on the lunar surface, calling it "one small step for a man, one giant leap for mankind."

  • 1991

    World Wide Web Launched

    Tim Berners-Lee introduces the World Wide Web to the public, setting the stage for the internet age.

    The WWW layer made the internet accessible and user-friendly, leading to massive global connectivity.

  • 2007

    Launch of the iPhone

    Apple releases the first iPhone, pioneering the modern smartphone and mobile computing era.

    The iPhone combined a mobile phone, an iPod, and an internet communicator into one device with a multi-touch interface.
